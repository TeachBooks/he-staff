# PhD council

The Faculty of Hydraulic Engineering has appointed a PhD Council dedicated specifically to our department. Primarily, the HE PhD Council is here to listen to you and your voice in the decision-making process of our department, faculty, and university. They are also here to promote interaction within the various sections of the department and further enable interdisciplinary research.
